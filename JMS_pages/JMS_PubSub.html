Publish Subscribe Messaging

The main goal of JMS Pub-Sub Model is that it allows several consumers to receive messages at a time.

Process:

Message is broadcasted to many consumers. It follows a one-to-many design.

  • When is it used?

    • Pub Sub Model is specifically used when message is sent from one producers to many consumers.

  • Advantages of Pub Sub Model:

    • 1. Follows a parallel processing method that improves scalability.

    2. Since all consumers have access to the messages, components are loosely coupled.

    3. The mechanism offes a wide visibility as all messages are accessible by all consumers.
